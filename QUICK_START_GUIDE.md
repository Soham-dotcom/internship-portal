# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - New Features

## Your Application is Running! ✅

Backend: `http://localhost:5000`

---

## 🎯 Two New Powerful Features

### 1️⃣ Company Search & Analytics
**URL**: `http://localhost:3000/analytics`

**What to do**:
1. Type a company name in the search box
2. Click on a company from results
3. See beautiful charts and student lists!

**Example searches**: Try "Google", "Microsoft", "Amazon", or any company in your database

---

### 2️⃣ Mentor Student Management
**URL**: `http://localhost:3000/mentor-edit`

**What to do**:
1. Type a student name or UID in search
2. Click "Review & Edit" on any student
3. Edit details across 4 organized tabs:
   - 📋 Profile (Name, UID, Branch)
   - 💼 Internship (Company, Role, Dates)
   - 📊 Status (Approval, Documents)
   - ⭐ Performance (Skills ratings)
4. Click "Save Changes"
